Frequently Asked Questions

To submit an article, first ensure you have a registered account. Navigate to the Author Dashboard by clicking "My account" and select "Submit New Manuscript". You will be guided through our 4-step submission wizard where you can upload your files, enter manuscript details, and review your submission before final approval.
Full-text access requires either purchasing the individual article, having a personal subscription, or accessing through an institution that has a subscription. If the article is marked as "Open Access", it should be freely available. If you believe you should have access via your university, please try using the Institutional Login page.
Our submission system accepts primary manuscript files in DOC, DOCX, and PDF formats. For figures and images, we recommend high-resolution EPS, TIFF, or JPEG files. Additional supplementary data can be uploaded in various formats including XLS and CSV.
Go to the Sign in page and click on the "Forgotten your password?" link below the login form. Enter the email address associated with your account, and we will send you instructions on how to reset your password.
